What is DevOps and DevOps Pillars?

About DevOps

DevOps is a set of practices that combines software development (Dev) and Operations (Ops) to improve the speed, quality, and reliability of software releases and deployments.

The DevOps Pillars

Culture: Embracing collaboration, openness, and psychological safety.
Practice: Implementing repeatable and reliable processes for software development and deployment.
Tools: Leveraging technology to automate tasks, improve productivity, and enhance quality.

Benefits of DevOps

  • Improved software delivery speed and frequency
  • Increased quality and reliability
  • Enhanced collaboration between developers and operations teams
  • Better visibility into the entire software development process
